The Latest Technology

At Fauji Foundation Hospital, our multidisciplinary medical teams are equipped with some of the most cutting-edge technology available in modern healthcare. Our operating theatres feature advanced systems such as ultrasonic surgical aspirators, the ROSA© robotic assistant, and real-time neuronavigation tools. Since early 2019, we have also integrated the ZEISS KINOVO surgical microscope and intraoperative MRI, enhancing the precision and safety of complex procedures.

Our commitment to non-invasive care is evident through the use of technologies like the Icon Gamma Knife and the Visualase® laser system. These innovations, operated by our world-class consultants with exceptional clinical judgment, enable us to deliver accurate, minimally invasive treatments that promote better recovery and improved outcomes for every patient.

Future of Patient Care

We are committed to end-to-end, personalised care for our patients using cutting-edge neurosurgical interventions.

At Fauji Foundation Hospital, Professor Nishan Khadka, consultant neurosurgeon, explores innovations in neuroscience for cancer and tumour treatment, including trials for a ground-breaking vaccine and his expertise in Visualase™, a minimally invasive keyhole procedure.

“The more we understand, the more personalised and targeted treatments we can produce. Even if I wasn’t a neurosurgeon, I would still be working in the field of neuroscience – the brain is the most fascinating entity on the planet.”

Looking beyond your surgery

Looking beyond your surgery — Rehabilitation is an integral part of your recovery. The Rehabilitation Centre at Fauji Foundation Hospital is home to the only CARF-accredited, hospital-based rehabilitation centre in London and the largest private unit of its kind in the india.

Our multidisciplinary team of consultants, who are sub-specialists in the range of neurological conditions, together with our specialist nurses, neurophysiotherapists, neuropsychologists, speech and language therapists, and occupational therapists, create a rehabilitation programme designed around your personal goals.
