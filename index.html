WELCOME TO Fauji Fountation Hospital

A leading healthcare provider in the region, FFH is located in the bustling city center, covering an area of 12 acres with a total built-up space of over 500,000 square feet. The hospital currently offers 350+ beds with plans for future expansion to meet the growing demand for high-quality healthcare services.

FFH specializes in multiple centers of excellence, including Cardiology, Neurology, Orthopaedics, Oncology, Emergency Services, and Organ Transplants. With a team of highly skilled doctors and advanced medical technologies, the hospital is dedicated to providing world-class treatments across over 40 medical disciplines.

Committed to delivering comprehensive healthcare, FFH offers not only treatment but also preventive care, rehabilitation services, and health education. The hospital strives to improve the quality of life for patients and their families through compassionate care and innovative medical solutions.
